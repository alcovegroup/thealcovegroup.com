Build A Home

Finding the value of your home can be a daunting task. There’s many things to consider: recently sold comparable homes in your area, active listings in your neighborhood, and the current state of the market. A meaningful evaluation takes due diligence. We take the time to thoroughly evaluate comparable homes and active listings by reaching out to listing agents that sold the homes or currently have the listings. We inquire about the history of the homes and why the seller’s wanted to move. Understanding why homes sold in your area will help you determine the true value of your home and can help increase your value in some cases. The Alcove Group offers a complimentary home evaluation. Take some time to fill out information about your home and an Alcove Home Consultant will be touch with you shortly to assist you.
